Data model. The grain is the source (source_id · type · owner · refresh_schedule · last_refresh · health · freshness · quality scores) with two children: a pipeline-job log (job · source · status · latency) and a lineage edge table (source → panel/dashboard via basis_id) that powers the lineage map and downstream-impact traversal. The defining role is that this is the foundation beneath every other dashboard: each panel in the Studio traces, through basis_id, to a source recorded here — so freshness, quality and especially the integrity / Compromised flag originate at this layer and propagate upward (the Compromised state begins as a failed integrity check here, then quarantines evidence in #6, decisions in #9 and rolls up in #12). The composite data-trust score is the single gate on whether the Studio's outputs can be relied on. ~30 worksheets across 8 sections. This completes the 13-dashboard Insights Studio catalogue.
