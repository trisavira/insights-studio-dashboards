Data model. Several reference grains: an HTA submission (submission_id · market · body · type · status · decision), a market access record (market · coverage · restrictions · status), and a payer-requirement matrix (requirement · hta_body/payer · importance · our_status) joined to the value-evidence inventory. The defining idea is that this surface scores evidence against a different bar than #5: HTA bodies weight comparative effectiveness, cost-effectiveness, real-world adherence and HRQoL — so a regulator-strong asset can still be access-thin. Those payer-specific gaps are routed to the Evidence Map (#5) and Book-of-Work (#6) as generation demand, while access barriers route to the field (#10). The economic model (ICER, budget impact) is HEOR-curated, not computed in Tableau. This is the payer/access axis ★ — a cross-cutting surface spanning all four pillars. ~30 worksheets across 8 sections.
