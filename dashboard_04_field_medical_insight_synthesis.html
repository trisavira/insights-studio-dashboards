Data model. Two grains feed one synthesis. Interaction grain (interaction_id · date · MSL · msl_team · HCP · specialty · tier · interaction_type · plan_flag · npse · insight_text · concept/theme · sentiment · criticality · launch_phase) from Veeva, and survey grain (respondent · wave · belief_statement · agreement · conviction · segment) from K&B. The defining Synthesize step is the theme model that resolves raw insight text into a managed theme set, assigns a state (Strengthening / Contested / Stable / Thin / Emerging — same vocabulary as the operator view), and computes confidence by triangulation across FM, social (#1) and congress (#2). Tableau renders the synthesis; the NLP theme/state model and the K&B-wave joins are the upstream work. ~30 worksheets across 8 sections.
