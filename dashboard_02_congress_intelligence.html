Data model. Two linked grains: abstract/study (study_id · congress · company · product · type · topic · late_breaker · ours_flag · attention_score) and congress insight/post (insight_id · congress · day · platform · author/KOL · specialty · study_ref · concept · sentiment · ae_flag · intent · engaged_flag). A congress master (congress · dates · tier · location) joins them for the portfolio and YoY views. This is a lifecycle surface — the same workbook serves pre-congress (planning: target abstracts, KOL/session targeting), live (daily pulse), and post-congress (this analysis) by switching the congress-status filter. ~30 worksheets across 8 sections; wire the congress + TA + product filters as one global parameter set.
