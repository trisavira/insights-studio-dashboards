Incumbent
Trikafta — >$10B/yr foundation
Durable real-world benefit; ~95% of US CF addressable after the Apr-2026 expansion. The standard of care for responsive genotypes.
Successor
Alyftrek — the self-cannibalisation ?
Once-daily, +31 mutations, lower sweat chloride, non-inferior FEV1 / superior QoL — priced 7% above Trikafta. New starts shift here; stable patients switch gradually. Extends the IP runway.
Unreached
The structural ~10% — and the failed answer ?
Nonsense/PTC genotypes make no full-length CFTR. VX-522 (inhaled mRNA) was discontinued in 2026; the untreated-minority lead answer fails.
Horizon
Genetic-medicine modality shift ?
mRNA / gene editing / tRNA could one day reach all genotypes and obsolete modulators — long, uncertain, delivery-limited. Sionna's NBD1 is the nearest external mechanism.
