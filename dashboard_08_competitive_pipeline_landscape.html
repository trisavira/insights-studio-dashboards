Data model. The grain is the competitor asset (asset_id · company · mechanism/MoA · development_phase · indication · threat_level · positioning scores) with a child catalyst table (asset_id · event_type · date · impact · likelihood) for the calendar and a TPP-scoring table (asset_id · dimension · score) for positioning. Much of the high-value content — threat levels, TPP scores, white-space, scenarios — is analyst-curated, blending external feeds (pipeline / trial registries, regulatory trackers, news) with internal judgement; the pipeline counts and catalyst dates are sourced, the strategic interpretation is human. SoV & market events draw on Congress Intelligence (#2). This is the external-axis ★ surface — the competitor-and-pipeline complement to the internal asset/stakeholder views. ~30 worksheets across 8 sections.
