Data model. The grain is the program (program_id · asset · TA · indication · lifecycle · priority · investment · health · status) — one level above the single-asset study book (#6). The defining mechanic is the roll-up: portfolio book-of-work, risk and pillar-activity counts are aggregations of the asset-level surfaces (studies from #6, evidence from #5, launches from #7, decisions from #9, field from #10), summarised per program/TA. The two signature analytics are investment-vs-priority alignment (how far each program sits from the strategy diagonal — the reallocation lens) and risk concentration (program × risk-type, with the Compromised integrity state carried up from #6 and isolated portfolio-wide). This is the leadership/portfolio axis ★ — a cross-cutting surface that turns the eleven operational dashboards into one allocation-and-prioritisation view. ~30 worksheets across 8 sections.
