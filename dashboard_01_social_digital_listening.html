Data model. One row = one insight record. Core fields: insight_id · date · platform · author/HCP_type · stakeholder_type · specialty · tier · indication · product · compound_mentioned · concept_tag(s) · sentiment · emotion_label · criticality_band · ae_flag · compliance_flag · intent · source_type · tl_category · dol_handle · influence_score · engaged_flag · impressions/engagement. The classifiers (ABSA, emotion, AE, off-label, intent, DOL influence) are the upstream models that turn raw posts into these fields — the dashboard is only as good as those. Wire the filter bar as one global parameter set; this assembles as ~28 worksheets across the 7 sections.
