Data model. The grain is the study (study_id · type[sponsored/ISR/RWE] · TA · indication · phase · lifecycle_stage · status · owner · risk_flag · risk_type · severity · state) with a child milestone table (study_id · milestone · planned_date · actual_date · SLA) and a decision/finance join (committed · spent · forecast; decision · forum · date · rationale). This consolidates iSTAR (study + milestone tracking, weekly), LDBOW (late-development book of work), MDG (data-generation plan), MSRMA (study decisions/reviews) and RFP (proposals), plus Finance. The Compromised state is a first-class status — not a risk severity — so integrity-flagged evidence is quarantined from every downstream surface (it never silently substantiates a claim in #5 or a decision in the operator view). ~30 worksheets across 8 sections; status/state drives conditional formatting throughout.
