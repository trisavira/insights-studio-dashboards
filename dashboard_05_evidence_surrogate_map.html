Data model. Two reference grains plus a linkage table. Endpoint (endpoint_id · type · TA · surrogacy_level · validation · regulatory_standing) and evidence item (study_id · endpoint · evidence_dimension · study_type · level_of_evidence · result · citation · asset/competitor), joined by a surrogate-linkage table (surrogate_endpoint · clinical_outcome · strength · validation · basis). The hard, distinctly-MA content here is curated, not computed: the surrogacy hierarchy and surrogate→outcome linkage strengths come from the literature and regulatory precedent and must be maintained by medical/scientific staff — Tableau renders the map but the scientific judgement is human. The incoming gap feed is the live join to #1/#2/#4; the MEG pipeline is the live join to #6. ~28 worksheets across 8 sections.
