Data model. Two grains. The field action (action_id · source_surface · owner(MSL) · account/HCP · priority · due · status · outcome) and the interaction (interaction_id · MSL · HCP · date · type · NPSE · insight · plan_flag) from Veeva CRM, joined to a target/alignment list (HCP · tier · region · MSL) for coverage. The defining feature is source-tagged actions: this is the convergence surface where engagement gaps (#3), coaching & insight-asks (#4), evidence delivery (#5), site support (#6), launch readiness (#7) and routed decisions (#9) all become concrete MSL tasks — and where field insights flow back up to #4, closing the loop. Coverage = covered/target by region×tier; NBA priorities come from #3's engagement-gap logic. ~32 worksheets across 8 sections.
