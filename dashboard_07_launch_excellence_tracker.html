Data model. Two grains. Launch (launch_id · asset · indication · market · phase · launch_date · timing[T-month] · readiness · status) and a child activity table (activity_id · launch_id · phase · workstream · owner · due_date · status · completion · critical_path). A phase model maps the five phases to T-months (T-24→T+12) with gate definitions; a readiness scoring rollup aggregates activity completion into workstream readiness (RAG-banded). The scorecard join activates post-launch (T0+) and carries the Launch-Scorecard / NPSE metrics. This consolidates the LET dashboard, Launch Scorecards and NPSE self-serve. Selecting a launch drives the phase spine, readiness and activity views; the post-launch scorecard only populates once timing ≥ T0. ~30 worksheets across 8 sections.
