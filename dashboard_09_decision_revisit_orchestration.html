Data model. The grain is the decision (decision_id · domain · owner · date · status · rationale · basis_id → source surface · confidence) with two child tables: a trigger table (trigger_id · type · condition_rule · state · armed_date) and a decision↔trigger link (which decisions each trigger watches). The defining Act-layer mechanic is the trigger engine: condition rules (new evidence, competitor catalyst, metric threshold, time-based, integrity, stakeholder shift) evaluate continuously, and a fired trigger moves its linked decisions to revisit-due. Provenance is the safety net — every decision carries a basis_id pointing at the surface that informed it (#3/#5/#6/#8); when that basis goes stale or, critically, Compromised ◆ (from #6), the dependent decisions are auto-flagged for revisit so no decision quietly rests on invalidated evidence. This is the surface that closes the Sense→Synthesize→Decide→Act loop. ~32 worksheets across 8 sections.
